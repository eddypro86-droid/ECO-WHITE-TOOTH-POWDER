Direction For Use

  • Wet your brush: Start with a clean, damp toothbrush.
  • Dip the brush in the powder: You only need a small amount to cover the tips of the bristles.
  • Brush for 2-3 minutes: Use gentle, circular motions just as you would with regular toothpaste.
  • Rinse: Spit and rinse your mouth thoroughly.
  • Routine: Brush twice daily for best results.

Caution & Care

  • For external use only. Do not swallow large amounts.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Store in a cool dry place to maintain powder consistency and ingredient potency.
